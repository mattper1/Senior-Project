Step 8: Install Storage

Materials Needed

  • SSD (Solid State Drive)
  • HDD (Hard Disk Drive)
  • 2 SATA Cables
  • Phillips Head Screwdriver

Instructions

  1. Install the SSD and HDD in the Case

    2. Place the SSD and HDD into their designated bays in the case or wherever you have space

  2. SATA Cables to the Drives

    3. Now you will be left with 2 SATA cables.

  3. Connect the SATA Cables to the Motherboard

    4. Locate the SATA ports on the motherboard and plug in the SATA cables.

  4. Final Result

    5. Congratulations! You’ve completed the assembly! Now, simply plug your power cable into the wall and connect it to your PSU. Your PC is ready to power on. However, keep in mind that you'll need to install Windows for your system to boot up properly. I’ll guide you through that process in the next section, "Essential Steps."
