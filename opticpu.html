Why Overclock Your CPU?

Overclocking your CPU can give your system a performance boost, particularly in CPU-bound tasks such as gaming, content creation, and multitasking. By increasing your CPU's clock speed, you can achieve better frame rates, quicker rendering, and overall improved system responsiveness.

Benchmarking CPU Performance

Cinebench is a commonly used tool for CPU stress testing. Cinebench allows you to benchmark your CPU's performance in real-world scenarios.

  1. Download Cinebench from the official website: Cinebench Download.
  2. Install and launch Cinebench.
  3. Select the CPU benchmark from the main menu.
  4. Start the test by clicking the "Run" button. Cinebench will stress test your CPU and provide a performance score.
