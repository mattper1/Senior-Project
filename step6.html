Step 6: Connect Cables

Materials Needed

  • Power Supply Cables
  • Motherboard Manual (for cable placement reference)

Instructions

  1. Locate Cable Ports on the Motherboard

    2. At this stage, you'll see a range of cables that need to be connected to the motherboard. Organize them for easy access.

  2. Identify CPU Fan Connector

    3. Locate the “CPU_FAN” port on the motherboard for the CPU cooler fan. This connector is typically labeled.

  3. Locate Chassis Fan Connector

    4. Identify the “CHA_FAN” or “SYS_FAN” connector on the motherboard for connecting any additional case fans.

  4. Prepare Front Panel Cables

    5. Gather the front panel connectors for items like power and reset switches, which will connect at the bottom of the motherboard.

  5. Consult Manual for F_PANEL Configuration

    6. Use your motherboard's manual to correctly place the front panel connectors. This ensures your power button, LEDs, and reset switch will function.

  6. Connect Front Panel Cables

    7. Align each front panel cable with the correct pins on the motherboard, referring to the manual for guidance.
